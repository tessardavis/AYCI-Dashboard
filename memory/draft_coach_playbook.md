# DRAFT AYCI Coach Playbook (starter pack)

> **How to use this**: Replace every `[PLACEHOLDER: ...]` with the real answer, then paste the whole thing into **Settings → Bot → Coach Playbook**. The bot will answer any question that clearly maps onto one of these entries and escalate anything else.
>
> Each entry is a question (the way a student would phrase it) followed by the answer the bot should give. Keep the answers short (1-3 sentences), factual, and link to a Circle space / URL if relevant.

## Live sessions & recordings

- **Where can I find the recording of a live session?** [PLACEHOLDER: Live session recordings are posted in the "Live Sessions" / "Recordings" space inside Circle, usually within 24h of the session. Look for the session date in the title. Direct link: <YOUR-CIRCLE-LINK>]
- **I missed yesterday's AMA — can I watch it back?** [PLACEHOLDER: Yes — AMA recordings go up in the "Live Sessions" space within 24h. The link is also pinned in the #announcements channel.]
- **Where's the calendar for upcoming live / bonus sessions?** [PLACEHOLDER: The full live session calendar is in the "Live Sessions" space inside Circle. You can also subscribe to the Calendly link here: <YOUR-CALENDLY-LINK>]

## Recorded answer videos

- **Where do I upload my recorded answer videos?** [PLACEHOLDER: Upload videos in the "Recorded Answers" space inside Circle. Tap "New post" → attach your video → tag the question topic. VIP/Private-tier members also have a dedicated <VIP-ANSWERS-SPACE> for private review.]
- **Can I upload answer videos on weekends?** [PLACEHOLDER: Yes — there's no day-of-week restriction. Videos can be uploaded any time, including weekends. Reviews happen Monday-Friday so a Sunday upload will be reviewed Mon-Tue.]
- **My video feedback was for someone else's answer — what happened?** Always escalate to the team. Never try to resolve mixed feedback yourself.
- **Where do VIP members upload videos?** [PLACEHOLDER: VIP / Private-tier members upload to the "Private Tier Videos" space inside Circle. This is separate from the general Recorded Answers space.]
- **Tips for recording my practice videos?** [PLACEHOLDER: Use your phone on a stable surface, good lighting on your face, audio clear (consider a wired headphone mic), and answer as if you're in the actual interview. Keep videos under 5 minutes per answer.]

## Question bank & member access

- **I'm VIP but can't access the interview question bank — it's asking me to upgrade.** [PLACEHOLDER: This is usually a sync delay between Stripe and Circle. Check your Circle profile shows the VIP role — if not, message us with your Stripe receipt number and we'll fix it within a few hours.]
- **I paid for the question bank but can't access it.** [PLACEHOLDER: Try logging out and back into the AYCI app. If still blocked, send us your Stripe receipt number and we'll re-grant access.]
- **Can I download the question sets?** [PLACEHOLDER: Question sets are view-only inside the app — downloading isn't supported (this protects the IP). You can take notes alongside.]
- **Where's the deep dive course material?** [PLACEHOLDER: Deep dive material is in the "Deep Dive" Circle space, accessible to VIP and Private Tier members. Direct link: <YOUR-LINK>]

## Cohort access & timing

- **The April cohort started but I just joined — can I catch up?** [PLACEHOLDER: Yes — all live session recordings are kept in the Recordings space, so you can catch up at your own pace. The community and coach calls are also open. Reach out if you need a coach call slot.]
- **My private tier ended but I still have videos to upload — what now?** [PLACEHOLDER: You can finish uploading your remaining videos in the Recorded Answers space for the next 2 weeks. After that, reviews are no longer guaranteed.]
- **Am I still on the private tier?** Always escalate so the team can check the Stripe subscription status.
- **When's the next cohort intake?** [PLACEHOLDER: The next intake is <DATE>. Join the waitlist at <WAITLIST-URL>.]

## Mock interviews & practice partners

- **Can you match me with a practice partner?** [PLACEHOLDER: Fill in the practice partner matching form: <URL>. Matches go out daily. If your interview is within a week, mention "urgent" on the form and the team will prioritise.]
- **My matched partner can't make time — can I be re-matched?** Always escalate. The team handles re-matching manually so it doesn't disrupt the other partner.
- **How do I book a mock interview?** [PLACEHOLDER: Use the Calendly link at <COACH-CALL-CALENDLY>. VIP and Private Tier slots are listed separately.]
- **Can I reschedule my mock interview?** [PLACEHOLDER: Use the reschedule link in your Calendly confirmation email (or contact the coach directly via Circle). Mocks need to be rescheduled at least 24h in advance.]

## Bookings & coach calls

- **My booking confirmation didn't have a Zoom link.** Always escalate — operational issue.
- **Do I have to use all my coach calls during this cohort?** [PLACEHOLDER: No — coach calls roll over for up to <X months> after your cohort ends, as long as you have an upcoming interview. Use the Calendly link to book whenever you're ready.]

## Tech issues & navigation

- **I'm getting a "something went wrong" error in the app.** Always escalate — the team needs to check logs.
- **How do I unfollow comments on a post in Circle?** [PLACEHOLDER: Open the post → tap the 3-dots menu in the top-right of the comment thread → "Unfollow comments". If that menu's not appearing, try the web version.]

## Cancellation, refunds & complaints

- **Refunds / cancellations / complaints**: Always escalate to the team. Never quote policy yourself.
- **I want to cancel my membership.** Always escalate.
